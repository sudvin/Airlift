## The Problem With the Current Site

The current site says "seamless solutions," "robust network," and "enduring partnerships." Every freight forwarder in India says this. Nothing on the site gives an exporter a reason to call Airlift instead of the next listing on IndiaMART.

What's missing:
- Specific HS chapter expertise (Chapters 63, 64, engineering goods, chemicals)
- Specific trade lanes (USA, Germany, Japan, China, France)
- Specific clients and industries (defense, government, retail, industrial)
- Any signal that Airlift knows customs law better than the competition
- A reason for overseas freight forwarders to reach out for bilateral partnership

---

## Two Audiences, One Site

The website needs to speak to two distinct visitors:

**Audience 1: Indian exporters/importers**
- Who: SME manufacturers and traders needing a customs agent + freight forwarder
- What they want to know: Can you handle my commodity? Do you know my HS chapter? Will you keep my shipment out of trouble?
- What converts them: Sector expertise, specific trade lanes, proof of problem resolution

**Audience 2: Overseas freight forwarders**
- Who: Freight agents in USA, Europe, Japan, Middle East looking for an India partner
- What they want to know: Are you reliable? Can I trust you with my client's cargo? Which ports do you cover?
- What converts them: Years in operation, FFFAI membership, port coverage, bilateral partnership model

---

**Current:** Generic logistics company based in Delhi.

**Proposed:** India's customs-first freight partner. 17 years of deep HS code expertise across engineering, textiles, chemicals, and defense — with offices in Delhi and Mumbai covering JNPT, NSICT, and ICD Tughlakabad.
