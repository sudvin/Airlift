## How Indian Exporters Find Freight Forwarders Today

**Referrals dominate. Everything else is a distant second.**

- Fellow manufacturers at Export Promotion Council events
- Shipping line reps
- Chartered accountants who handle export docs
- Google → IndiaMART listings (phone book, not a marketplace — no ratings, no quoting)
- FFFAI member search exists but is a credentialing tool, not matchmaking

**What SME exporters care about when choosing:**
- Lane-specific experience ("Are you strong on the USA lane?")
- Knowledge of DGFT, CBIC, ICEGATE, LC requirements
- Cost transparency (hidden local charges are top complaint)
- Real-time visibility (they manually chase forwarders for updates)
- Documentation accuracy (errors cause penalties)
- Most run 2-3 pilot shipments before committing

**Digital platforms — limited India penetration for SME B2B:**
- Cogoport was the closest India-focused marketplace — now shrinking (revenue -26% YoY FY24, 1,000 → 240 employees)
- Freightos/iContainers: minimal SME B2B penetration in India
- Flexport: enterprise only
- Shiprocket/Credlix: eCommerce parcels and trade finance — not relevant for ACL

## How Overseas Forwarders Find Indian Network Partners Today

**Paid membership in freight networks is the primary channel.**

| Network | Size | Cost | Trust Level | Notes |
|---------|------|------|-------------|-------|
| WCAworld | 13,000+ members, 196 countries | ~$5,000/yr | Medium | India roadshows in Delhi, Kochi, Mumbai. Common complaint: "waste of time and money, don't take complaints seriously" |
| JCTrans | 340,000 registered, 12,000 paid, 179 countries | Lower | Low-Medium | No built-in comms (email only). Documented fraud: Egyptian member ran $150k in debts across 12 members in 6 months. Has blacklist. |
| Conqueror | One member per port/city, 138 countries | High | High | Annual conference with 32 structured 1:1 meetings. Delhi member cited exclusivity as the reason to join. |
| Cargo Connections | 87 companies, 50 countries | High | Very High | ISO certified, highly selective |
| Parnity | 75+ countries | Medium | High | Digital-first, 1-click RFQ, built-in chat, financial background checks |
| FreightNet | 28,000+ listed | Free | Low | Open directory, no vetting |

**Why experienced operators distrust these networks:**
- Spent decades building bilateral relationships — new directory contacts carry unknown risk
- One bad partner (non-payment, cargo damage) costs more than the membership fee
- Common frauds: non-payment while using cargo as leverage, wire transfer hijacking, cloned company profiles, partial delivery with cargo held for ransom

**What a good bilateral partnership looks like:**
Multi-year relationship, documented shipment history in both directions, clear 30-day payment terms, FIATA membership verified, one direct contact, reference checks from mutual contacts.

## Competitive Landscape — Who's Trying to Solve This

**Digital forwarders competing WITH traditional operators (not helping them):**
- Cogoport, Intoglo, Pazago, LinkedLogi — built to let exporters bypass or reduce dependence on CHAs
- These are the threat, not the opportunity

**AI tools for customs/HS codes:**
- **India-specific:** Only Intoglo has an AI HS Code Scanner (photo → code). Pazago has duty calc.
- **Global:** Digicust (Germany), Avalara (launched AI tariff classification Sept 2024), CustomsAI, xNova — none India-first
- WCO neural network: 93.9% accuracy on difficult subheadings
- Industry claim: AI cuts clearance time by 60%
- **Gap: No AI tool built for Indian CHA workflow (ICEGATE, Indian notifications, Chapter-specific law)**

## The Four White Spaces (Nobody Is Filling These)

1. **Growth platform for small Indian freight forwarders/CHAs** — no tool helps a 17-year-old Delhi/Mumbai forwarder get found online, build a credible digital profile, or generate qualified leads. This segment is invisible to digital buyers.

2. **Vetted India-specific bilateral partnership network** — WCA and JCTrans are too large and shallow. Conqueror is exclusive but small. No one has combined: verified trade history + escrow/payment protection + deep India port coverage.

3. **AI tools built for the CHA's daily workflow** — HS classification, document generation, ICEGATE filing assistance — designed for a 10-50 person shop, priced for small operators. This space is open.

4. **Discovery layer for overseas forwarders → specific Indian operators by port, commodity, trade lane** — an overseas agent looking for a Nhava Sheva partner specializing in pharmaceutical exports has no efficient, trustworthy way to find and vet one.
